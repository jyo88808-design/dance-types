Types of Dance

Bharatanatyam

A classical Indian dance form known for its grace and storytelling.

Kathak

Originating from North India, Kathak is famous for spins and footwork.

Hip Hop

A modern street dance style full of energy and freestyle moves.

Kuchipudi

A classical dance from Andhra Pradesh, combining fast rhythms and dramatic storytelling.

Odissi

One of the oldest Indian classical dances, known for graceful movements and sculpturesque poses.

Ballet

A Western classical dance form emphasizing elegance, technique, and storytelling.

Salsa

A lively Latin dance style performed with a partner, full of rhythm and energy.
